About TDII
Shaping the Future of Fashion Design

At TDII – The Design Institute of India, we are committed to nurturing creativity and building strong design foundations for aspiring fashion professionals. Our institute focuses on blending innovative thinking with practical skills, helping students explore the evolving world of fashion and design.

With expert guidance, industry-oriented training, and a strong emphasis on creativity, we empower students to transform their ideas into impactful designs. At TDII, we don’t just teach fashion — we inspire confidence, originality, and success in every student’s journey.

Our Courses
Learn, Create & Excel in Fashion Design

At TDII – The Design Institute of India, we offer a wide range of design programs crafted to suit every stage of a student’s creative journey. From foundational learning to advanced specialization, our courses in Fashion, Interior, Jewellery, and Textile Design combine creativity with practical skills. With flexible durations ranging from 6 months to 4 years, our programs focus on hands-on training, industry exposure, and portfolio development to help students build successful careers in the design industry.

  • Undergraduate Courses: B.DES, BFA, B.Sc, B.Voc (3–4 Years)
  • Postgraduate Programs: Advanced learning & specialization in design fields
  • Diploma Courses: 6 Months to 3 Years flexible programs
  • Specializations: Fashion, Interior, Jewellery & Textile Design
  • Practical Learning: Hands-on training, live projects & workshops
  • Career Support: Portfolio development & placement assistance
